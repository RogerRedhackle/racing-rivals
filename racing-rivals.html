How scoring works ▼
Win: Full SP odds as points (e.g. 10/1 winner = 10 pts)

Place: SP ÷ number of official place positions (e.g. 10/1 horse in 3-place race = 3.33 pts)

Day Multiplier: Mon 1.0× · Tue 1.1× · Wed 1.2× · Thu 1.3× · Fri 1.4× · Sat 1.6× · Sun 1.5×

Hard Cap: Maximum 25 pts per day regardless of SP × multiplier.

Scoring anchor: Official Starting Price — never shop odds.
How streak bonus works ▼
Score in 3 consecutive days (any Win or Place result) and receive a +2 bonus point reward. Resets if you miss a scoring day. Unused trade swap also earns +1 loyalty point at week end.
